How to Play

Goal

Listen to a mystery sound, then recreate it using three sliders.

The Three Dimensions

Pitch -- How high or low the note is (like hue in color).

Brightness -- How muffled or sharp the tone is (like saturation).

Space -- How dry/close or wet/spacious it sounds (like value/brightness).

How It Works

1. Tap play to hear the target sound (2x, then 1 replay).

2. Drag any slider to hear your version live -- it plays while you drag!

3. Match your memory of the target. Submit when ready.

Scoring (20 per round, 100 max)

Each dimension scores 0-7 based on accuracy. Closer = more points.
